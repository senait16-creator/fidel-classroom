How It Works

1
Each level has 3 letter families

A letter family is a row of 7 characters that share the same base sound. Your current level shows which 3 families to work on.

2
Get a streak of 20 in the matching game

Match letters to their sounds. Get 20 correct in a row without missing. Miss one and your streak resets to zero — take your time.

3
Submit your handwriting

After passing the streak, submit a photo or drawing of your handwriting. Your captain reviews it and approves or asks you to try again.

4
Clear all 3 families to advance

Once all 3 families are cleared a button appears to submit for teacher approval. Your teacher signs off and your team moves to the next level.

!
Your whole team moves up together

Every team member needs to clear all 3 families before the team advances. If a teammate is stuck, help them — your progress depends on theirs.
